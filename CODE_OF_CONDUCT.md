# RedSec Learn Code of Conduct

RedSec Learn should be a respectful, practical place to study security. Contributors are expected to be welcoming, constructive, and focused on safe learning.

## Standards

- Use inclusive and respectful language.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect legal and ethical boundaries in all security content.
- Do not encourage harassment, unauthorized testing, disruption, or misuse.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or participation that does not match these standards.
