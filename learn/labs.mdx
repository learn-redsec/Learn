title: Labs
description: Small, contained exercises for authorized practice.
---

Labs are where the concepts become muscle memory. Use local environments, course-provided targets, or intentionally vulnerable systems that clearly allow practice.

## Lab Checklist

Before starting:

- Confirm the lab is authorized.
- Reset the environment if needed.
- Open a notes file.
- Record the goal of the exercise.

During the lab:

- Save commands and important output.
- Explain each result in your own words.
- Pause when behavior is unclear and narrow the next test.

After finishing:

- Summarize the path to the solution.
- Record what you would try differently next time.
- Keep any cleanup or reset steps with the notes.
