---
title: Reconnaissance
description: Safe research habits for mapping authorized lab targets.
---

Reconnaissance is the process of learning what exists before deciding what to test. In this course, reconnaissance should stay focused on authorized labs and local practice environments.

## What To Record

- Hostnames, IP addresses, and ports in scope.
- Application paths and visible features.
- Software names and versions when they are intentionally exposed.
- Interesting errors, headers, redirects, or authentication boundaries.

## A Simple Notes Format

```text
Target:
Scope:
Observation:
Evidence:
Question:
Next step:
```

Good reconnaissance is not about collecting the most data. It is about collecting useful, permitted data and turning it into a testable next step.
