---
title: Rules of Engagement
description: How to keep practice authorized, scoped, and responsible.
---

Only practice on systems you own, systems you administer, or labs that explicitly permit testing. If the rules are unclear, stop and clarify them before continuing.

## Before Testing

Confirm:

- The target is in scope.
- The testing window is allowed.
- The techniques you plan to use are permitted.
- You know where to report problems or questions.

## During Testing

Stay inside the approved environment, avoid unnecessary disruption, and keep enough notes to explain exactly what happened. Do not touch real third-party systems while following these materials.

## After Testing

Write down what worked, what failed, what evidence supports the result, and what a defender could change to reduce risk.
